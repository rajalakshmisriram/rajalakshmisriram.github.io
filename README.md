# Rajalakshmi Sriram's Resume

Welcome to my resume website! This is a one-page website showcasing my resume, including my education, experience, skills, and more. The website is designed to reflect my passion for neuroscience and my dedication to a future career in medicine.

## About the Website

This website is designed to be:
- **Professional**: Clean and elegant design to make a strong impression.
- **Eye-catching**: Engaging visuals that highlight my achievements.
- **Creative**: Reflecting my passion for neuroscience and medicine.

## Features

- **Smooth Scrolling Navigation**: Easily navigate through different sections using the menu at the top.
- **Responsive Design**: The website is designed to be viewed on both desktop and mobile devices.
- **Distinctive Fonts**: Different fonts are used to differentiate between titles and dates for better readability.

## How to View

You can view the resume website by visiting the following link:

[My Resume Website](rajalakshmisriram.github.io)

## Sections

The website includes the following sections:
- **About Me**: A brief introduction to who I am and my interests.
- **Experience**: My professional and volunteer experiences.
- **Education**: Details about my educational background.
- **Skills**: A list of my key skills.
- **Publications**: My published works.
- **Courses**: Relevant courses I have completed.
- **Awards & Honors**: Recognition and awards I have received.
- **Languages**: Languages I speak and my proficiency levels.
